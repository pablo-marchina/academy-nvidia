# Evidence Policy

## Rules

- Every factual claim must carry at least one source.
- Evidence objects must preserve URL, source type, confidence, and collection time.
- Weak signals must be labeled as inference or unverified.
- Quotes should be concise and directly tied to the claim they support.

## Do not do

- Invent sources
- Inflate confidence
- Present hypotheses as facts
- Ignore contradictory evidence
