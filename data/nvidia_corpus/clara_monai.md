## Description
NVIDIA Clara is a healthcare AI platform for medical imaging, genomics, and
sensor-based care. MONAI (Medical Open Network for AI) is an open-source
framework for deep learning in medical imaging. Together they provide
compliance-ready tools for healthcare AI development and deployment,
including support for HIPAA and data privacy requirements.

## Keywords
Clara, MONAI, healthcare, medical imaging, compliance, HIPAA, genomics, privacy

## Use Cases
- Developing medical imaging AI models with MONAI frameworks
- Deploying compliant healthcare AI applications on NVIDIA infrastructure
- Accelerating medical image segmentation and classification
- Ensuring data privacy and regulatory compliance in healthcare AI
