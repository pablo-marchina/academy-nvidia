# NVIDIA Isaac

## Product
NVIDIA Isaac

## Gaps Addressed
- robotics_need

## Description
NVIDIA Isaac is a platform for developing, simulating, and deploying AI-powered
robots. Isaac Sim provides physically accurate simulation environments for
training and testing robotic models. Isaac ROS offers GPU-accelerated ROS 2
packages for real-world robot deployment.

## Keywords
Isaac, robotics, robot, simulation, ROS, autonomous, manipulation, navigation

## Use Cases
- Training robot manipulators in physically accurate simulation
- Simulating robot navigation in complex environments
- Deploying GPU-accelerated perception pipelines on real robots
- Reducing robotics development time through simulation-first workflows
