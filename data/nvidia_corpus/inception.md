# NVIDIA Inception

Product: NVIDIA Inception
URL: https://www.nvidia.com/en-us/startups/

NVIDIA Inception is NVIDIA's startup program for companies building with AI,
data science, accelerated computing, and GPU-enabled products. Inception can be
used as program-fit context when a Brazilian startup has public evidence of AI
or GPU product maturity.

Keywords: NVIDIA Inception, startup program, AI startup, GPU, acceleration, go-to-market
