## Gaps Addressed

## Description
NVIDIA Omniverse is a platform for building and operating physically accurate
3D simulations and digital twins. It enables real-time collaboration,
physically based rendering, and physics simulation for industries ranging
from manufacturing to robotics. Omniverse supports USD (Universal Scene
Description) as its foundation.

## Keywords
Omniverse, simulation, digital twin, 3D, USD, rendering, physics, visualization

## Use Cases
- Creating digital twins for industrial simulation and monitoring
- Simulating physical processes for product development
- Building training environments for AI models
- Visualizing and analyzing complex 3D data in real time
