# Epic 28 - Documentation Mining & Final Product Backlog

**Date:** 2026-06-11

## Summary

Epic 28 mined the accumulated project docs, contracts, plans, examples, scripts,
API, frontend, pipeline, RAG, evaluation, tests, and local data artifacts to
consolidate a final product backlog.

## Outcome

- `docs/54_final_product_backlog.md` is the productization source of truth.
- Demo-first surfaces are identified as replacement or hardening targets.
- Historical plans/docs are no longer active product guidance unless merged into
  live docs.
- The recommended next technical epic is Product Backend Foundation: persisted
  entities, product API contracts, analysis-run lifecycle, and explicit degraded
  dependency states.

## Principle

Demo paths are useful for validation, but the product path must operate on
persisted startups, evidence, analysis runs, scores, gaps, mappings, briefs,
reviews, exports, and readiness checks.
