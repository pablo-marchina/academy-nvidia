# Final Product Backlog as Productization Source of Truth

**Decision:** Use `docs/54_final_product_backlog.md` as the active source of
truth for final product backlog and documentation pruning decisions after Epic
28.

**Date:** 2026-06-11

## Context

The project accumulated many plans, demo docs, contracts, evaluations,
roadmaps, examples, and Obsidian notes. Some are live guidance, some are
historical, and some describe demo paths that should not define the final
product.

## Decision

- Product work should be derived from the P0/P1 items in
  `docs/54_final_product_backlog.md`.
- Historical plans and demo docs should not guide implementation unless their
  content has been merged into a live doc or converted into backlog/test/contract
  items.
- README, ROADMAP, EVALS, AGENTS, DECISIONS, contracts, and the final backlog
  are the live product guidance set until superseded by a later explicit plan.

## Status

Accepted for Epic 28.
