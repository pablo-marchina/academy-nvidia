# EVALS

## Classification

AI-native classification should be correct in at least 80% of manually reviewed cases.

## RAG

Generated answers should cite correct sources in at least 90% of reviewed cases.

## Recommendation

Recommendations must map gap to NVIDIA technology with a clear technical justification.

## Briefing

The final briefing should be useful for commercial outreach in at most two minutes of reading.

## Core Metrics

- Precision@k
- Recall@k
- Faithfulness
- Context relevance
- Citation correctness
- Business usefulness
