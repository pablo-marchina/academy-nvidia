# SOURCES

This file defines preferred public sources for startup research and evidence validation.

## Level 1 - High Confidence

- Official startup websites
- Official startup blogs
- Public careers pages
- Public founder profiles
- Endeavor Brasil
- Abstartups
- 100 Open Startups
- Valor Economico
- Brazil Journal
- NeoFeed

## Level 2 - Medium Confidence

- StartSe
- Distrito
- Latitud
- Cubo Itau
- ACE Startups
- Bossa Invest
- Anjos do Brasil
- Darwin Startups
- Liga Ventures
- WOW Aceleradora
- InovAtiva Brasil
- Exame Startups
- Startups.com.br
- PEGN
- Meio & Mensagem
- Mobile Time

## Level 3 - Lower Confidence

- General directories without editorial standards
- Secondary reposts without original sourcing
- Old blog posts with unclear maintenance
- Community summaries lacking citations

## Source Use Rules

- Prefer Level 1 sources whenever possible.
- Use Level 2 to triangulate claims and enrich context.
- Treat Level 3 as weak signals, not strong proof.
- No claim should be accepted without recording URL, source type, and access date.
